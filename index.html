Our Legacy

About Colchuck's

Est. 25+ Years

A Leavenworth cornerstone, Colchuck's has existed in some form for more than twenty-five years. Owned by father and son duo, Carl and Gavin Evans, our mission is simple: To take pride in creating a wonderful experience for all who come through our doors.

We offer a wide selection of Pacific Northwest and coastal craft beers and ciders, as well as a variety of German and European imports. Serving house-made German schnitzels and sausages, and pouring hand-crafted cocktails for all to enjoy.

Our kitchen is committed to quality, sourcing fresh, local ingredients from the bounty of the Pacific Northwest. From our hand-breaded schnitzels to our artisan burgers, every dish is prepared with care and passion, bringing a taste of modern Germany to your table.

We know you have plenty of options, and we appreciate you choosing us. We look forward to meeting you!
